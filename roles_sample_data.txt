--- ROLE 1 ---
Name: Super Administrator
Description: Full access to all system modules, including developer settings, system user management, and audit logs.
Status: Active

--- ROLE 2 ---
Name: Donor Relations Manager
Description: Primary access to the Donor List and financial reports. Responsible for managing sponsorship records and communication.
Status: Active

--- ROLE 3 ---
Name: Social Worker
Description: Access to the Children List and reports. Responsible for updating child profiles, case studies, and monitoring progress.
Status: Active

--- ROLE 4 ---
Name: Data Entry Clerk
Description: Limited access to input new donor and child records. Cannot delete entries or access system settings.
Status: Active

--- ROLE 5 ---
Name: Board Member
Description: Read-only access to the dashboard, donor summaries, and children reports for oversight purposes.
Status: Active

--- ROLE 6 ---
Name: Finance Officer
Description: Specialized access to donor financial records, tax receipts, and budgetary reports.
Status: Active

--- ROLE 7 ---
Name: Temporary Volunteer
Description: Restricted access for short-term projects or internships. Currently disabled.
Status: Inactive
